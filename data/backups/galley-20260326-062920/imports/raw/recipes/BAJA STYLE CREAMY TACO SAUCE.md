In-N-Out Spread (Authentic Style)

Ingredients:

1/2 cup mayonnaise

3 tbsp ketchup

2 tbsp sweet pickle relish

1 tbsp finely diced onion

1/2 tbsp white vinegar

1/2 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp paprika

Pinch of salt

Instructions:

Whisk mayo and ketchup together until smooth.

Stir in relish and diced onion.

Add vinegar, sugar, paprika, and salt.

Chill 30 minutes before serving.

Here’s the combined full recipe from the two screenshots:

Big Tasty Sauce

Ingredients

1/2 cup mayonnaise

2 tbsp BBQ sauce

1 tbsp ketchup

1 tbsp yellow mustard

1 tbsp finely minced onion

1 tsp smoked paprika

1/2 tsp garlic powder

1/2 tsp onion powder

1/2 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp white vinegar

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp black pepper

1 tsp liquid smoke


Method

1. Prepare the base
In a mixing bowl, add:

1/2 cup mayonnaise

2 tbsp BBQ sauce

1 tbsp ketchup
Whisk until smooth.



2. Add flavor ingredients
Mix in:

1 tbsp yellow mustard

1 tbsp minced onion

1 tsp smoked paprika

1/2 tsp garlic powder

1/2 tsp onion powder
Stir well.



3. Balance the flavor
Add:

1/2 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp white vinegar

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp black pepper
Mix thoroughly.



4. Add smoky flavor
Add:

1 tsp liquid smoke
Stir again.



5. Chill
Cover and refrigerate for 30–60 minutes so the flavors blend properly.



Storage

Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator

Shelf life: 5–7 days


Best Uses

Burgers

Grilled chicken sandwiches

Fries dipping sauce

BBQ wraps

Smash burgers
