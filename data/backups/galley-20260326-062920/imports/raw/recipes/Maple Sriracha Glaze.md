Maple Sriracha Glaze

Ingredients

1 cup maple syrup

2 tbsp sriracha

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp butter

1 tsp apple cider vinegar

½ tsp garlic powder

½ tsp smoked paprika

How To Make

Add all ingredients to a small saucepan.

Heat on medium-low heat.

Stir constantly for 3-4 minutes until slightly thick.

Toss with wings or brush over grilled chicken.

Great for wings, fried chicken, or grilled burgers.

2 Garlic Parmesan Wing Sauce Ingredients

½/ cup butter (melted)

1½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

3 garlic cloves (minced)

1 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp Italian seasoning

½ tsp garlic powder

½ tsp black pepper

1 tsp salt

1 tbsp chopped parsley

How To Make

Melt butter in a small pan.

Add olive oil and minced garlic.

Cook 1 minute until fragrant.

Stir in Parmesan, seasoning, salt, and pepper.

Toss hot wings in the sauce.

Perfect for buffalo wings and crispy chicken tenders.
